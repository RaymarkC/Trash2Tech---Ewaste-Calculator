Who We Are

Trash2Tech is a student-driven project designed to spread awareness about electronic waste (e-waste) and its environmental impact. Through our interactive E-Waste Calculator, we encourage responsible disposal and recycling of gadgets and devices.

Our Mission

To educate communities on the importance of e-waste management and provide digital tools that make sustainability simple and accessible.

Our Vision

A cleaner future where technology and the environment coexist in harmony.
